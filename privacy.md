# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-25

## Summary
Table Fixer runs inside Google Docs. The add-on edits tables only in the document you open. The add-on does not sell data.

## What data the add-on accesses
The add-on may access:
- The content of the Google Doc you run it on
- Table structure and formatting in that document

## What data the add-on stores
The add-on does not store your document content.

The add-on may store:
- Basic settings you choose inside the add-on, if you add settings later

## What data the add-on shares
The add-on does not share your data with third parties.

## Analytics
The add-on does not use tracking pixels or third-party analytics.

## Google account and permissions
Google shows a consent screen before you use the add-on. Google controls your sign-in. The add-on only requests scopes needed to run its features.

## Data retention
The add-on does not keep copies of your Google Docs. Google Drive keeps your files under your account.
