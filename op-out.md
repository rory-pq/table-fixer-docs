# Draft Tester Opt-Out (Table Fixer)

If you do not want to use Table Fixer as a draft tester, you can opt out at any time.

## 1) Uninstall the add-on
1. Open Google Docs.
2. Click Extensions.
3. Click Add-ons.
4. Click Manage add-ons.
5. Find Table Fixer.
6. Click Uninstall.

## 2) Revoke Google account access
1. Go to your Google Account.
2. Open Security.
3. Open Third-party access.
4. Find Table Fixer.
5. Click Remove access.
